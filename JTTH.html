Welcome to Journey to the Heavens

Welcome, aspiring cultivator, to the Journey to the Heavens Character Creator.

This guided process will help you forge your path through the realms of cultivation, from humble mortal beginnings to the heights of power.

Before you begin, we recommend reviewing:

  • JTTH Wiki - For detailed information about the world, cultivation, and game mechanics
  • Trello Board - For updates, rules clarifications, and additional resources

Your journey begins with a single step. Are you ready to walk the path of cultivation?

Stat Assignment Guide

How to Determine Stats

Choose one of two methods to assign your starting stats:

  • Standard Array: Assign the values 1, 2, 3 to your six stats (each value used twice)
  • Roll/Manual: Roll 6d3 and manually enter the results

Power (PWR)

Measures: Physical strength and raw power

Important for: Melee damage, carrying capacity, physical techniques

Agility (AGI)

Measures: Speed, reflexes, and dexterity

Important for: Evasion, initiative, ranged attacks, movement speed

Vitality (VIT)

Measures: Constitution, endurance, and health

Important for: Hit points, stamina, resistance to injury and poison

Mental Strength (MTL)

Measures: Willpower, focus, and mental fortitude

Important for: Resisting mental attacks, cultivation comprehension, technique learning

Qi Control (QIC)

Measures: Ability to manipulate and control Qi

Important for: Technique effectiveness, Qi efficiency, cultivation speed
